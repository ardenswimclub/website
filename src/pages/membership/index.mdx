## NO Half Price / Half Season Memberships

In past years, we offered half-price pool memberships beginning July 15 through the end of the summer season. As announced last season, because many of our operating expenses occur at the start of the summer and we need confirmed membership numbers to properly plan and budget, we will no longer be offering mid-season discounted memberships. 

We appreciate your understanding as we work to manage costs responsibly and maintain a high-quality experience for all members.
