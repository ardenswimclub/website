# 🏊 Arden Swim Club Membership

Memberships are available for **individuals, couples, and families of all sizes**.

We also offer **discounts for eligible members** — see below for details.


## **✅ How to Join**

All members must register annually using our secure membership software:  

<Link href="https://www.esoftplanner.com/v3/planner/login.php?access=0dG81LSVxNmo65bCzW57uZyIpg==">👉 Log in to eSoft</Link>

⚠️ **Important:** **Before visiting the pool**, you **must** complete your online membership registration.

If your registration is not finalized, our lifeguards will not be able to check you in — this is a safety and security requirement.

### Steps to Register

1. **Log in** (**or Sign Up)** to your eSoft account .

   * Forgot your login or password? We can help you recover it — just reach out.

2. **Choose your membership level** based on your household size.

   * Options available for individuals, couples, and families.

   * **Bondholders** in good standing may select the *Bond Membership* to apply their discount.

3. **Click “Sign Up”** next to the membership that fits your needs.

4. **Optional:** Add guest passes to your account.

5. **Proceed to payment:**

   * Pay online via credit card, or

   * Select **"Mail Check"** and contact us to make alternative payment arrangements.
